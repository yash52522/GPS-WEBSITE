About Gondia Public School

Established in 1998, Gondia Public School (GPS) is the premier and the most renowned CBSE institution in Gondia District, sprawled across  5 acres of picturesque landscape. With affiliation number 1130047, GPS offers education from Pre-Nursery to standard XII, under the visionary leadership of Chairman Prof. Arjun Budhe and Secretary Dr. Indira Sapate. The members of managing committee are renowned for their academic prowess and contribution in the field of education and sports. GPS has garnered accolades across Central India and Maharashtra.
